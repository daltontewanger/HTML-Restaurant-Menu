About Us - Origin Story

Welcome to H-Thai-ML, where coding meets cuisine in any of our four locations! Born in San Diego from a fusion of flavors and a passion for programming, our restaurant is a byte-sized haven for foodies and tech enthusiasts alike. We don't just serve HTML, but an exquisite array of Thai dishes meticulously crafted to satisfy your palate and your inner geek. From spicy noodles that pack a punch to curries that'll make your taste buds do the happy dance, we believe in delivering a dining experience that's both 'byte'-ful and 'delight'-ful. Join us in indulging in a flavorful escapade that leaves your stomach full and your HTML-enthusiast soul content!
